Online Banking FAQs

1. How do I enroll in online banking?

You can enroll by visiting our website and clicking on the "Register" or "Sign Up" button. Follow the steps to verify your identity and create a username and password.

2. Is online banking secure?

Yes, we use advanced encryption and multi-factor authentication to ensure your information stays safe and secure.

3. What should I do if I forget my password?

Click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page and follow the prompts to reset your password using your registered email or phone number.

4. Can I transfer money to other banks?

Yes, you can easily transfer funds to accounts at other banks through our online banking portal using the "External Transfer" feature.

5. What if I see suspicious activity on my account?

Immediately contact our customer support and freeze your account through the app or website to prevent further unauthorized access.

6. Are there fees for using online banking?

Online banking is free for most basic services. Some transactions like wire transfers or international payments may incur small fees.
